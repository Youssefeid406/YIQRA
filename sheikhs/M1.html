Biography

Mohamed Tarek is an young Egyptian famous video content creator, Singer/Musician who was born in a Muslim family on October 19, 1989 in Egypt and also raised in there. He became known for his work on YouTube, Instagram and Facebook. This popular influencer found huge response and success on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram.
